Skue: Bulk Order Form

Fast ordering,
built for Shopify

A spreadsheet-style order form for B2B wholesale and retail storefronts. Give dealers, logged-in customers, or any shopper a faster way to buy — on Shopify Plus, Advanced, and Basic.

View on Shopify App Store

Bulk Order Form

A spreadsheet-style grid that lets dealers add dozens of products to cart in seconds. Variant selectors, quantity rules, and search built in.

MAP & Margin Display

Show retail prices alongside dealer cost so buyers see their margin at a glance. Powered by Admin API for accurate pricing on B2B catalogs.

Starter Orders

Pre-built templates and CSV upload let new dealers place their first order with a curated product mix — no guesswork needed.

Quick Reorder

Previous orders are saved automatically. Customers reorder in one click with quantities pre-filled — perfect for recurring B2B and retail purchases.

Create Profiles

Launch multiple order forms tailored for B2B accounts, logged-in customers, or public visitors — each with its own products, pricing, and display rules.

Delivery Windows

Let buyers choose from available delivery dates at checkout. Configure windows by day of week, lead time, or custom schedule to match your fulfillment workflow.
